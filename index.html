About Jeremy

A traveler who
lives the work

I'm a lifelong traveler who spent the past three years living and working around the world as a digital nomad. I started as a classic backpacker and over the years refined my taste, preferences, and budget into a mix of city, food, and nature-focused travel.


I pride myself on connecting with locals and immersing myself in order to have the most authentic and fulfilling travel experiences — which also happens to be the best way to get restaurant recommendations.


Whether you want an unforgettable long weekend in Mexico City (complete with my custom restaurant and bar map created while living there), a dive trip to the world's most stunning coral reefs, or a stress-free honeymoon — I have got you covered.

South America
